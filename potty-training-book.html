Frequently Asked Questions

What age is best to start potty training?

Most children show signs of readiness between 18 months and 3 years old. The book covers how to recognize readiness signs and avoid starting too early or too late.

Does this method work for both boys and girls?

Absolutely! The book includes specific tips for both boys and girls, addressing the unique challenges each may face.

How long does potty training typically take with this method?

While every child is different, many parents report success within 1-3 weeks using Tracy's gentle, consistent approach.

What makes this different from other potty training books?

Tracy's 40+ years of hands-on experience with real children sets this apart. Every tip has been tested in her daycare, plus it specifically addresses home-daycare coordination.

Does this work in daycare settings?

Yes! Tracy still runs her own daycare in Ottawa and uses these exact methods daily. The book includes special sections on group potty training.

What if my child resists or has setbacks?

The book has an entire chapter called "Potty Push Back Blues" that deals with resistance and regression using gentle, understanding approaches.
