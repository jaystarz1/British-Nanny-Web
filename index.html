What is NNEB Certification?

The NNEB (National Nursery Examination Board) qualification is Britain's gold standard in nursery nurse training - a prestigious government-regulated certification that sets me apart from ordinary childcare providers.

I received my NNEB certification following a two-year intensive full-time program at Mable Fletcher College in Liverpool (1981) that involved comprehensive study, practical placements, and national examinations covering:

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About The British Nanny's Daycare

What makes The British Nanny's Daycare different from other Ottawa daycares?

We offer authentic British early years education from an NNEB-certified nanny with 40+ years experience. We specialize in allergy-safe care, expert potty training (I wrote the book!), and maintain a TV-free environment with maximum 5 children for personalized attention.

How do you handle children with food allergies?

We maintain a completely nut-free facility and follow safe food handling practices for all children. I will administer EpiPens if authorized by parents and have extensive experience managing severe allergies. We follow strict protocols, check all labels, and have emergency plans in place. Many parents choose us specifically for our allergy management expertise.

What is your potty training approach?

I use the proven methods from my published book "The British Nanny's Guide to Potty Training." My approach is patient, positive, and adapted to each child's readiness. I coordinate with parents to ensure consistency between home and daycare.

What areas of Ottawa do you serve?

While located in Barrhaven, we serve all of Western Ottawa including Nepean, Kanata, Bells Corners, Westboro, Britannia, Bayshore, Crystal Beach, Centrepointe, and Merivale. Parents drive from across the region for our specialized care.

What are your hours and rates?

We're open Monday to Friday, 7:30 AM to 5:00 PM. Our rate is $55 per day with no drop-in service - we maintain committed families only for consistency and quality care.

What ages do you accept?

We care for children from 1 year old to Junior Kindergarten entry (approximately 4 years old). Each age group receives age-appropriate activities and individualized attention.

Are you a licensed daycare?

We operate as a legal home daycare in Ontario caring for 5 children. While not licensed by the Ministry, we maintain the highest professional standards through my NNEB qualification from the UK - a rigorous two-year government-regulated certification in childcare. With over 40 years of experience and as a published childcare author, I provide exceptional care that exceeds many licensed facilities. Our small group size ensures personalized attention and quality that larger centers simply cannot match.

What does "British nursery practices" mean?

Children experience authentic British early years traditions including structured learning with play, emphasis on manners and etiquette, outdoor "garden" time, story time traditions, and yes - they naturally pick up delightful British expressions!

How does the TV-free environment work?

We focus on hands-on learning, creative play, books, outdoor activities, and real-world experiences - just traditional childhood activities that foster imagination and social skills.

You don't use TV but do you use any other screens?

Yes, I use an iPad in the daycare for music and body movement activities like dancing and large motor movement development.

Do you provide meals and snacks?

We provide healthy, allergy-safe snacks. Parents typically provide lunch. All food is carefully managed with our strict allergy protocols, especially in our nut-free environment.

How do I secure the September opening?

Contact us immediately to schedule a visit! With only one spot available and high demand for quality home daycare in Ottawa, this opening won't last long. Call or email today.

Can I tour the daycare?

Absolutely! We encourage parents to visit, meet me, see our space, and learn about our program. Tours are by appointment - contact us to schedule your visit.
